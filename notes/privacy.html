Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 26, 2023

Thank you for using Gajju Notes. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our web application.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal data. Gajju Notes is designed as a client-side application. This means that all the notes you create and edit are processed and stored entirely within your web browser on your device.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage feature to save your notes. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers or databases controlled by us.

  • Your data persists even if you close the browser.
  • Clearing your browser's cache or local storage will delete your notes.

3. Third-Party Services

This application loads resources from the following third-party services, which may collect standard access logs (such as your IP address):

  • Google Fonts: Used for typography.
  • Famral.com: Used to host the application icon.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
